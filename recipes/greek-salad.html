Greek Salad

Description

Authentic Greek salad - classic Mediterranean cuisine

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Green bell pepper
  • 1/2 Red onion
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives
  • 2 T Red wine vinegar
  • 2 T Olive oil
  • 2 T Oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Feta
  • Za'atar

Instructions

  1. Make vinaigrette : Mix together red wine vinegar, olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper
  2. Chop vegetables : slice cucumbers and cut into quarters. Cut tomatoes into quarters. Thinly slice onions. Chop green pepper. Chop olives
  3. Add vegetables into bowl. Add vinaigrette. Mix everything together
  4. refrigerate for one hour
  5. To serve, scoop 1/2 cup salad into a bowl. Top with slice of feta and sprinkle on Za'atar
