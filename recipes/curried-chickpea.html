Curried Chickpea Salad

Description

An awesome curried chickpea salad recipe

Ingredients

  • 15 oz canned chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayo
  • 2 T Red Onion
  • 2 T Fresh Parsley
  • 2 T Finely chopped pickles
  • 1 T Curry powder
  • 3 T Raisins
  • Squeeze of lime
  • 1/2 t dijon
  • Salt and peppper

Instructions

  1. Literally pu everything into a bowl
  2. Literally mash everything together
  3. Literally put food in your mouth
